Moduli (美都里)
Meduli is a bar singer. Every week, she and her lover Ale would see each other across town. Sometimes they meet in sparsely attended cinemas, sometimes in crowded parks, sometimes by a river on the outskirts of town.
2025 · 15 min · Digital · Stereo

About

Wenyu Zhang (TUDONI) (b. 1995, Chengdu) is an independent film director and former actor. He has moved across multiple artistic forms before arriving at cinema as a way to observe, approach, and articulate the essence of human emotion.

张文瑜（TUDONI），1995年出生，现居成都。曾为演员，现为独立电影导演。我穿过了很多方式，确定通过电影去观察、到达、描绘人类情感的本质。
